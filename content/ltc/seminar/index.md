Design-by-contract is a very successful concept to achieve modularity
in program and also system verification. An active communities has
established around the goals to establish bridges between these two
notions of contracts and to exchange ideas between the various
application for formal contracts. 

Following Dagstuhl and Lorentz seminars on the topic, we will continue
with a series of online presentations.

**Information on how to subscribe to the mailing list for the seminar and more information on the topics and dates will follow soon here.**
